## Our Models

### [pico-type](https://huggingface.co/eulogik/pico-type) 🔍

Tiny byte-level multi-head content classifier — ~1.5M params, ~209KB ONNX, <6ms inference.
Classifies any content into 7 categories from raw bytes.
